Preproduction Stages - Square Mask
----------------------------------

1. Material for outside and inside has been measured and cut out.

2. The two pieces of material have been pinned together to the elastics.


Production Stages - Square Mask
-------------------------------

1. The material has been stitched together with the elastics.

2. The pipecleaner has been stitched in to the top of the mask.

3. The pleats have been created and stitched.


Completion: the elastics have been threaded through the beads and tied or stiched together.
